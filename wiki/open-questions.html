Open Questions & Research Backlog

A running list of hypotheses, unknowns, and directions worth chasing. Add to this whenever a conversation raises something unresolved.

Last updated: 2026-07-18

Aim 1 — Prevention / fixing the malformed protein

  • Is the C1GALT1/Cosmc galactosylation defect genetic, epigenetic, or micro-environmentally driven — and therefore correctable? high value, under-drugged
    Partial answer (Conv 2): serum Gd-IgA1 is ~50–70% heritable but common variants (C1GALT1, C1GALT1C1) explain only a minority; most of the defect is regulatory/acquired — Cosmc promoter hypermethylation and IL-6/STAT3 & TGF-β cytokine effects — which suggests it is reversible in principle. See deep-dive.
  • Could a protein/biologic clear existing Gd-IgA1 or immune complexes, not just reduce new production?
  • How durable is APRIL blockade? Does Gd-IgA1 rebound after withdrawal?
  • Is there a mucosal/microbiome intervention that reduces the mucosal immune trigger upstream of APRIL?

Aim 2 — Reversal of damage

  • Which complement pathway (alternative vs. lectin) dominates injury, and does it vary by patient (biomarker-stratifiable)?
  • Is early fibrosis reversible? What signals would drive myofibroblast de-differentiation or nephron repair? the hard problem
    Partial answer (Conv 2): yes within a reversible window — human proof-of-concept (pancreas-transplant regression), myofibroblast reversal via BMP7/anti-TGF-β, podocyte/progenitor repair — but destroyed nephrons don't regrow. See deep-dive.
  • Do upstream (Aim 1) + downstream (Aim 2) combinations give additive or synergistic benefit?

Cross-cutting

  • What non-invasive biomarkers (serum Gd-IgA1, anti-glycan antibody titers, complement fragments) best track response and could replace biopsy?
  • Could structural/computational tools (AlphaFold, ESM, docking) design a binder to the Gd-IgA1 hinge or to C1GALT1 to test these hypotheses in silico?
