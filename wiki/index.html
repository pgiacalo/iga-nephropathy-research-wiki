IgA Nephropathy Research Wiki

A living knowledge base on IgA nephropathy (Berger's disease) and promising treatments.

Last updated: 2026-07-18

This wiki captures the significant discussions, discoveries, and open questions from our work on IgA nephropathy (IgAN). It is organized by topic, not chronologically — the Conversations log provides the dated, chronological index and links into the relevant topic pages.

Two research aims drive this project:
  1. Prevention — fix the malformed protein (galactose-deficient IgA1, Gd-IgA1) or stop it from becoming malformed.
  2. Reversal — repair or reverse kidney damage that has already occurred.
