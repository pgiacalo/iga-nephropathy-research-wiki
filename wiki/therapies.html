Recent status changes (since kickoff)

  • Sibeprenlimab — FDA accelerated approval (VOYXACT, Nov 2025); 2-yr VISIONARY eGFR positive Jul 2026. Was "Phase 2/3."
  • Atacicept — FDA accelerated approval (TRUTAKNA, Jul 2026). Was "Phase 3."
  • Iptacopan — converted to full FDA approval Jul 2026.
  • Telitacicept — China approval is now specifically an IgAN indication (2026).
  • Narsoplimab — Phase 3 ARTEMIS-IGAN failed and was discontinued (2023). Removed from active pipeline.
  • New agents added — Atrasentan (Vanrafia, approved Apr 2025), Povetacicept (PDUFA Nov 2026), Felzartamab (anti-CD38, Ph 3), Ravulizumab (Ph 3), Finerenone (anti-fibrotic).
  • Pegcetacoplan — clarified: Phase 2 for IgAN; its approval is for the different indications C3G / IC-MPGN.
