Glossary

Key terms and abbreviations used across the wiki.

Last updated: 2026-07-18

TermMeaning
IgANIgA nephropathy (Berger's disease) — the most common primary glomerulonephritis worldwide.
Gd-IgA1Galactose-deficient IgA1 — the malformed protein with under-galactosylated hinge O-glycans; central to disease.
GalNAcN-acetylgalactosamine — the sugar left exposed on the hinge when galactose is missing; the autoantibody epitope.
C1GALT1Core 1 β1,3-galactosyltransferase — the enzyme that adds galactose to the IgA1 hinge glycans.
CosmcMolecular chaperone required for C1GALT1 folding/activity; its dysfunction causes under-galactosylation.
APRILA Proliferation-Inducing Ligand — cytokine driving IgA-producing plasma cells; a key upstream drug target.
BAFFB-cell Activating Factor — related cytokine; co-targeted with APRIL by dual inhibitors.
MesangiumStructural/immune tissue of the glomerulus where immune complexes deposit ("clog").
ComplementInnate immune cascade; the alternative and lectin pathways drive IgAN injury.
MASP-2Mannan-binding lectin-associated serine protease 2 — effector of the lectin complement pathway.
RAASRenin-angiotensin-aldosterone system — blockade (ACEi/ARB) is background renoprotective therapy.
eGFREstimated glomerular filtration rate — primary measure of kidney function/progression.
ProteinuriaProtein leakage into urine — key biomarker and clinical trial endpoint in IgAN.
